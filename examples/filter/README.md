### `visualize_filter.py`

Visualize effect of filter on a Dirac-delta peak, i. e.  a single filled 
element.

### `visualize_lengthscales.py`

visualize length scale violation, i. e. places in the design where details are
smaller than a sphere of a given radius which resembles the desired lengthscale.

Short demonstration to check volume conservation after filtering.
