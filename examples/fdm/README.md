### `allencahn_fdm.py`

Solves Allen-Cahn equation via finite differences and explicit Euler. Only
for demonstration and comparison with FEM.

### `cahnhilliard_fdm.py`

Solves Cahn-Hilliard equation via finite differences and explicit Euler. Only
for demonstration and comparison with FEM.

### `cahnhilliard_pseudo.py`

Solves Cahn-Hilliard equation via a pseudospectral method and explicit Euler. Only
for demonstration and comparison with FEM.
